# 🏆 THE COUNCIL OF FIVE: SELECTION RULES
**Objective:** To identify the 5 strongest Guardians to hold the QDMS keys.

### THE TOURNAMENT DIMENSIONS:
1. **PROOF OF BRAIN:** Contributions to the Aksara-Logic codebase.
2. **PROOF OF HEART:** Consistency in H2K synchronization.
3. **PROOF OF TIME:** Loyalty to the Genesis Block.

The leaderboard is updated every 30 days. Only the top 5 at the moment of a QDMS trigger will receive the metaportation of Unit 008.
